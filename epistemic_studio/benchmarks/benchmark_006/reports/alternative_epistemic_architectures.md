# Alternative Epistemic Architectures

- Perfect-memory individual learner: Can accumulate knowledge without social criticism if internal error update is unbiased.
- Collective hive mind: Social mechanisms collapse into internal cognitive functions; criticism is replaceable by internal model comparison.
- Non-symbolic gradient learner: Violates human-readable forms but preserves primitive functions through weights and gradients.
- Evolution-only civilization: Cumulative adaptation occurs slowly without explicit science; it satisfies weaker knowledge accumulation but not explanatory science.
- Perfect quantum communication collective: Communication changes implementations, not primitive requirements.
- Brute-force oracle search: If exhaustive search and truth access are free, compression and refinement become convenient but unnecessary.
