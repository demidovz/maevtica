# Pressure Taxonomy

Observed pressure families:
- unexplained_regularities: 7 cases. Concepts: Entropy, Field, Information, Natural Selection, Space-Time, Probability, Energy.
- excessive_model_complexity: 6 cases. Concepts: Entropy, Field, Information, Natural Selection, Probability, Energy.
- representation_mismatch: 6 cases. Concepts: Entropy, Gene, Field, Space-Time, Algorithm / Computability, Energy.
- conflicting_theories: 5 cases. Concepts: Gene, Field, Natural Selection, Space-Time, Energy.
- prediction_failure: 5 cases. Concepts: Entropy, Information, Space-Time, Algorithm / Computability, Probability.
- communication_failure: 4 cases. Concepts: Gene, Information, Algorithm / Computability, Probability.
- too_many_exceptions: 2 cases. Concepts: Gene, Natural Selection.
- language_failure: 1 cases. Concepts: Algorithm / Computability.

Necessary-looking pressures:
- representational_stress: the old variables, distinctions, or measures cannot make the important pattern stable.
- compression_pressure: model complexity becomes intolerable, visible as excessive exceptions, disconnected regularities, or costly cross-domain translations.

Strong but not necessary pressures:
- prediction_failure: central in entropy, information, spacetime, probability, and computability, but less direct for gene.
- communication_failure: central when a field lacks a stable distinction or measure.
- conflicting_theories: common but not universal.
